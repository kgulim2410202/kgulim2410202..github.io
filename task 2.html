Hamilton, a revolutionary Broadway musical, was created by Lin-Manuel Miranda. Scored in hip-hop, R&B, and traditional show tunes, it narrates the life story of Alexander Hamilton, one of the Founding Fathers of the United States. The musical is based on Ron Chernow's biography about Alexander Hamilton and follows the life of the orphaned immigrant from the Caribbean who becomes one of the most important figures in the American Revolution and in the early U.S. government.

The story starts as he arrives in America, shows how he shapes the nation into a financial system, develops friendships and rivalries (especially with Aaron Burr), marries Eliza Schuyler, and ends with his tragic death in a duel. It talks about ambition, legacy, love, and betrayal all under war with the colonies.

How much history you know, how well informed you are about music, or how much you love the underdog- Hamilton is for you!
